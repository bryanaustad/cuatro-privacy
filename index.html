Privacy Policy for Cuatro Game

At Cuatro, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your information when you use our mobile game app. We want to make it clear that Cuatro does not collect or store any personal data from its users.

Information Collection

Cuatro does not collect any personal information or data from users. We do not ask for, access, or store any details such as your name, email address, or location. The app does not require any login or registration, and there is no personal user information stored on our servers.

Data Usage

As the app does not collect any personal information, there is no data being used for advertising, analytics, or any other purposes.

Third-Party Services

Cuatro does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect or process user data. We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising tools that would track your activity.

Security

Since we do not collect or store any user data, we do not need to implement any specific security measures related to user information. However, we take general security measures to ensure the app functions correctly.

Children's Privacy

Cuatro is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here. Please review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
